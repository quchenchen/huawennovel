# The Cursed Bond — Official Blurb

I'm Sera Voss, the lowest wolf in the border pack.
And the last Silver Vael — the only bloodline that can bind and judge an Alpha King.

"I reject the bond," the Alpha King said coldly.

At first, I saw it only as a shame.
An unexpected rejection.
But I was wrong.
It was protection.
He pushed me away to keep me from Cassius's blade.

Cassius Hale massacred my entire bloodline thirty years ago.
He thought he buried the last one.
He was wrong.

This time, I will stand by the King's side.
To avenge my kin.
To uncover the truth.
I will make them pay.

He can reject the bond.
He cannot reject the binding.
